Hostel Curfew:
All residents must be inside the hostel premises by 9:30 PM. Late entry requires prior permission from the warden.

Visitor Policy:
Visitors are allowed only in the common areas between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM on weekends.

Noise Regulations:
Silence hours are to be observed from 11:00 PM to 6:00 AM. Loud music is strictly prohibited.

Room Maintenance:
Residents are responsible for the cleanliness of their rooms. Weekly inspections will be conducted by the warden.

Mess Timings:
Breakfast is served from 7:30 AM to 9:00 AM. Dinner is served from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM.
